- India: Data related to Tata, light trucks, medium-duty/heavy-duty trucks and buses update becomes from monthly to every 3 months from April 2020. Monthly data is an estimate. - India: Monthly sales volume by models including PVs, UVs/MPVs is updated twice in a month (mid-month and around 20th). During the first update (mid-month), combined volume for all models under a single segment is denoted with {Segment}, under the model column. This combined volume is further sorted and data for respective models is updated on the second update (around 20th). For details click here.
- MarkLines "Search By Model/EV/etc." function identifies companies as being affiliated when the investment ratio exceeds 50% (excluding Chinese brands and other exceptions), so Mitsubishi Motors is not included in the Renault-Nissan Group.
